Section 02 / Business Case + Proposed Solution

The Problem You're Solving — and How Reeco Closes It.

Three pain points from your team. Three direct answers.

01
The Problem

Disconnected Systems, Manual Handoffs

Your AP and payments workflows run through separate, unconnected tools today — invoices flow into HIA, then payments go out through a different system entirely, with no bridge between them. As your own team put it, the block is "having systems that don't necessarily talk to one another."

The Answer

One Connected Flow, Start to Finish

Reeco connects purchasing, receiving, and AP automation directly to HIA with a direct API integration — invoices arrive coded and matched, with no manual re-entry between systems. We're now extending that same connected approach to payments, so the full order-to-pay cycle can run through one platform instead of three.

02
The Problem

Purchasing Split Across a Fragmented Marketplace

Your properties buy from a wide mix of suppliers, from large broadliners to independent, offline vendors — with no single place to price-shop across all of them at once.

The Answer

One Curated Marketplace, Per Property

Reeco builds a property-specific marketplace using your existing supplier relationships and pricing — broadliners and offline/mom-and-pop suppliers alike — so your teams can price-shop across every vendor in one cart. As an Avendra GPO partner, Reeco can build your marketplace around your Avendra-approved suppliers, so purchasing stays aligned to your GPO agreement.

03
The Problem

AP Automation That Still Needs a Human in the Loop

Most AP automation tools break down the moment an invoice has multiple GL codes or multiple pages — someone still has to step in and fix it by hand.

The Answer

99% Straight-Through Coding

Reeco's AP automation reaches a 99%-plus success rate on invoice coding with no human intervention, learning from each property's coding history so it gets more accurate the longer it's in use.

Section 03 / The Platform

What's Included.

Four modules. One login. Built for hospitality back-of-house from the ground up.

Purchasing & Receiving

Full visibility and control across F&B, housekeeping, engineering, and service agreements. All your suppliers in one connected place — existing partnerships, pricing, and agreements honored. Mobile receiving lets your team check in orders on the spot; Reeco handles missing items and credits automatically.

AP Automation

Every invoice — organized, AI-coded by line item, and ready to sync with HIA. What used to take 7–10 minutes per invoice takes about 20 seconds. The coding learns over time, so it gets faster and more accurate the longer you use it.

Inventory

Always up-to-date pricing, easy audit setup, and a mobile-friendly count experience. Slider-based counting for bar and alcohol inventory; open the app, count by tapping through the list, and your report is ready instantly.

Culinary Management

Menus connected to recipes. Clear visibility into food cost by dish — what's making money, what's costing more than it should, and where to adjust before the next menu cycle. Works alongside your Toast POS.

Section 07 / FAQs

Questions We Expect — Answered Directly.

If something's missing, email Zander and we'll add it before your walkthrough.

How do you onboard our existing suppliers — do we lose our current pricing or relationships?
No. We get your supplier logins and configure live pricing directly from them — broadliners and independent, offline suppliers alike. If a supplier has no online ordering, we stand up an email PO flow so orders still reach them the way they do today. Pricing updates automatically from each new invoice, so what you see in Reeco reflects your actual negotiated rates.
Will this actually match what we're getting from Folio's pilot?
Yes. Our 90-day pilot is $0 per month, the same structure you're running today, with a one-time $500-per-property onboarding fee that's fully credited back if you move forward — so there's no new monthly cost to evaluate us head-to-head.
Does Reeco give us budget and forecast visibility with HIA, the way it does with Profit Sword or M3?
Yes. Budget-versus-actual by GL account is live at checkout for HIA customers today. It shipped to production in May 2026 and is already live.
Can we use Reeco for payments if we're on HIA?
Not yet, for the specific pairing of HIA and payments — that combination hasn't been built for any customer to date. Our Corpay-powered payments module is live today for customers on M3. Given the disconnect your team flagged between AP and payments today, we'd like to make Lark our first HIA-paired payments design partner as part of this pilot.
How hard is implementation, and how long does it take?
Reeco does not require a dedicated full-time resource to manage implementation. We configure HIA integration credentials at kickoff and build your curated marketplace property by property — we'll walk through the exact sequencing for your 3 pilot properties at your walkthrough call.
